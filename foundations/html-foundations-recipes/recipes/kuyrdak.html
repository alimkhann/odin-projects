Kuyrdak

Description

Kuyrdak is a hearty, traditional roast typically made immediately after
a harvest or slaughter. It features a mix of meat and offal (liver, heart,
and kidneys) saut√©ed with onions and often potatoes to create a rich,
savory dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg Mixed meat and offal (lamb or beef)
  • 2 Large onions
  • 3-4 Potatoes, cubed
  • 200g Fat or oil
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Heat fat in a heavy cauldron (kazan).
  2. Add the meat and offal, frying until browned.
  3. Add chopped onions and continue to fry until soft.
  4. Add cubed potatoes and a small amount of water or broth.
  5. Cover and simmer until the potatoes are tender and
    the liquid has reduced to a thick sauce.
