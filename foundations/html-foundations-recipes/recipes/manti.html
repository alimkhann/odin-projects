Manti

Description

Manti are large, succulent steamed dumplings filled with finely chopped meat and onions.
They are characterized by their thin, delicate dough and the rich juices trapped inside, often
served with sour cream or a spicy tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • 500g Ground lamb or beef
  • 3 Large onions, finely chopped
  • 400g Flour
  • 1 Egg
  • Salt, pepper, and cumin

Steps

  1. Knead flour, water, and egg into a firm dough; let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Mix the meat with onions and spices. The onion ratio is high to ensure the filling stays juicy.
  3. Roll out the dough thin and cut into squares.
  4. Place a spoonful of filling in each square and pinch the corners together to seal.
  5. Steam in a multi-tiered steamer (mantovarka) for 40-45 minutes.
